    What we Do

    We the team of LAKSHYA FOUNDATION provide the feeling of social conscience among the people of INDIA. Having the power to improve the lives of others is , to many people , a privilage , and one that comes with its own sense of obligation. Acting on these powerful responsibility is a great way to reinforce our own personal value and feel like we are living in a way that is true to our own ethical beliefs. "THE MORE WE GIVE , THE HAPPIER WE FEEL". Keeping this in mind, we have taken an initiative to come forward and help those people , especially children who really want somebody`s help to create our future and listen those voices which is always negated by our highly and well settled societies. We work on various projects like FEED FOR THE NEED , CLEAN INDIA GREEN INDIA , PETS CARE , WOMEN IMPOWERMENT etc. And we want everybody to become a part of this initiative and serve INDIA with LAKSHYA FOUNDATION. Our lakshya is to listen those voices and make INDIA better in every aspects. We have taken this pledge and we welcome everybody to donate something for our nation.
